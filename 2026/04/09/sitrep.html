The two-week ceasefire brokered by Pakistan (effective Apr 8) has immediately fractured over whether Lebanon is covered. Iran insists Lebanon is an inseparable part of the deal. US and Israel explicitly say Lebanon is excluded. Pakistan PM Sharif originally stated the ceasefire applied "everywhere, including Lebanon." European diplomats are calling for Lebanon's inclusion.

Mahdi Mohammadi, Iranian national security adviser, warned that only "limited hours remain" to preserve the ceasefire. The IRGC Aerospace Commander echoed the warning.
